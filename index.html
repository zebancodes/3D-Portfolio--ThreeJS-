*Record Scratch* Yep, that's me, you're probably wondering how I got here. It all started back in 1998, a child was born on a Tuesday afternoon in November. One drop of the head later; I woke up 23 years later, a student in Computer Science, Studying at Franklin University in Columbus, Ohio a year away from graduating.

I originally intended to stop there, but once I got close to graduation, I decided I wanted to continue and develop my skills in Computer Science at Franklin University.
